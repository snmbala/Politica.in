Parliment Constitutions in Tamilnadu (39)

Andipatti

Andipatti is constituency No. 198 of Tamil Nadu legislative assembly. It is situated in Theni district and comes under Theni Lok Sabha constituency. It is an open constituency. According to data with the Election Commission of India, it has 2,09,097 registered voters. It comprises 1,04,933 male and 1,04,164 female registered voters.
